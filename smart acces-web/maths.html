MATHS

UNIT-I LOGIC AND PROOFS

Propositional Logic – Propositional equivalences – Predicates and quantifiers – Nested quantifiers – Rules of inference – Introduction to proofs – Proof methods and strategy – Normal forms –Applications to switching circuits.

UNIT-II COMBINATORICS

Mathematical induction – Strong induction – The pigeon hole principle – Recurrence relations – Solving linear recurrence relations using generating functions – Inclusion and Exclusion Principle and its applications.

UNIT-III GRAPHS

Graphs – Graph terminology and special types of graphs – Subgraphs – Matrix representation of graphs and graph isomorphism – Connectivity – Eulerian and Hamilton graphs.

UNIT-IV ALGEBRAIC STRUCTURES

Algebraic systems – Semigroups and monoids – Groups – Subgroups Homo morphisms – Normal subgroup and coset – Lagrange’s theorem – Definitions and examples of Rings and Fields.

UNIT-V LATTICES AND BOOLEAN ALGEBRA

Partial ordering – Posets – Lattices as Posets – Properties of lattices – Lattices as algebraic systems – Sublattices – Direct product and Homomorphism ; Boolean algebra – Stone’s representation Theorem.
